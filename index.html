Predictive Stock exchange

Use our powerful AI models to predict stock prices and make informed investment decisions.

Effective use of historical data

We use a hybrid of the three most effective machine learning algorithms for stock exchange prediction:

  • LSTM (Long Short Term Memory)
  • ESNs (Echo State Networks)
  • LightGBMs (light gradient-boosting machines)

    • ESNs help in accounting for the chaotic dynamics of the stock market.

    This acts as the base, on top of which sentiment analysis and AI strategies are performed.

    Sentiment analysis

    We perform sentiment analysis of public news and posts on Twitter to better understand the public perception of a stock.

    We use image-to-text and audio transcription algorithms to filter out the irrelevant news and then use ML libraries to analyze it.

    Feedback loops

    We post polls on our platform to get user feedback on their perspective of stock news.

    We feed this data back to our model to better train it, especially with respect to the performance of our predictions.

    AI strategies

  • Personalized algorithms: Users can teach their personalized strategies to our automated bots. This improves the accuracy of the predictions made for that user.
  • Learning top investors: We analyze the patterns followed by top investors and train our AI models to learn from them.

    • Outcomes

    After all the analysis is done, the expected output from our platform is to predict if the user shall buy a stock or not.

    Automated trading

    With the user's consent, we can also automate the trading of stocks based on our predictions.

    This can be done for modifiable prices and time.

    Security

    We implement stop losses to prevent unforeseen situations.

    All trades are redirected to a trusted stockbroker.
